Daily Quote

How to Play:
Guess a famous quote in 10 letters or less!

Choose a letter and press ENTER to have all of them revealed on the game board. A maximum of 10 letters can be chosen before you must solve the quote. Pressing SHOW SPACES BETWEEN WORDS will count as a letter used but is not available in Hard Mode. There will be no periods but commas and apostrophes are available choices. The quote can be solved at any time but once the GUESS THE QUOTE button is pressed you cannot return to choosing letters. Once in the GUESS THE QUOTE mode, enter the letters in sequential order to complete the quote. If you need to make a change, press the appropriate box and choose the correct letter from the keyboard. Once all letters are entered the GUESS THE QUOTE button will now change to SOLVE

Game board for: "I THINK, THEREFORE I AM"

Spaces between words will not appear at the end of a line, only at the beginning of the second row. Choosing the SHOW SPACES BETWEEN WORDS button shows the spaces on the game board.

After choosing these characters , , I , E and M the board will look like this. If a letter appears more than once, all instances will be revealed.

When a letter is not in the quote, it will be removed from the keyboard.

In the GUESS THE QUOTE Mode

The empty boxes will be shaded blue. Letters can now be entered in order with the yellow hightlight or you can click any blue box and enter that letter.

Once you have entered all letters to complete the quote, press SOLVE

Points

  • 10 points for each letter revealed (except the space)
  • 50 bonus points for every unused letter
  • 100 bonus points for not using the space
  • 500 points for solving correctly
