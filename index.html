Daily Quote

Guess a famous quotation with only 10 letters.

Choose indivdual letters and press ENTER to have them revealed in the game board. A maximum of 10 letters can be used before you must solving the quotation. Showing SPACES BETWEEN WORDS counts as a turn but is not available in Hard Mode. There are no periods but commas and apostrophes will count as a letter choice. The quote can be solved at any time but once the GUESS THE QUOTE is pressed you cannot return to choosing letters. Once in "Guess Mode", enter the letters in sequential order to complete the quote. The GUESS THE QUOTE button will change to SOLVE .

Spaces between words will not appear at the end of a line, only at the start beginning with the second row. Choosing the SHOW SPACES BETWEEN WORDS button removes the box outline on the game board.

After choosing these characters , , I , E and M the board will look like this. If a letter appears more than once, all instances will be revealed.

When a chosen letter is not in the quotation, it will be blacked out on the keyboard.

GUESS THE QUOTE

The boxes with missing letters will be shaded blue. Each blue box is now a button. Letters can be entered in order following the yellow hightlight or you can click any blue box and enter that letter.

Once you have entered all the letters to complete the quote, press SOLVE .

Points

  • 10 points for each letter revealed
  • 50 bonus points for every unused letter
  • 100 bonus points for not using the space
  • 500 points for solving correctly
